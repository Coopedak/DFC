Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website form, phone, text, or email, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and manage charter bookings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to booking inquiries and confirm trip details
  • To communicate weather updates and trip logistics
  • To send follow-up information you have requested

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is only used for the purposes described above.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand site traffic and improve our pages. These tools collect anonymous usage data and do not identify you personally.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form may be processed by Formspree. Their privacy policy governs data submitted through the form. We also link to external sites (Facebook, Michigan DNR, NOAA) which have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete any personal information we hold by contacting us at booking@davesfishingcharters.com or calling 231-672-0399.
