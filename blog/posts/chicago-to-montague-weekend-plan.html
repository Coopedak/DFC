Chicago to Montague Weekend Fishing Plan

Published: March 6, 2026

Quick answer. Drive Friday fish Saturday keep Sunday flexible for weather or easy travel home.

Sample weekend plan

  • Friday: drive in and get licenses done
  • Saturday: early launch from Montague Municipal Boat Launch
  • Sunday: optional backup day or drive home

Why this plan works

Less rushing in the morning and better chance to fish the best first light window.

FAQ

Can we do one day only? yes plenty of groups do and it works fine.

Do we need to bring gear? nope we provide it.
