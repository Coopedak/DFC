Best Time for Salmon Near Montague (Month by Month)

Published: March 6, 2026

Quick answer. Usually June through August is easiest for most groups but spring and fall can be very good too.

Month by month

  • April-May: cold water shifts. good fish if weather lines up
  • June-July: steadier windows and family friendly conditions more often
  • August: bigger fish chances. morning timing matters alot
  • September: still can be solid but windows tighten up

Booking note

If your date is fixed book early. if your flexible we can find better water days easier.

FAQ

Do first timers do okay in summer? Yes usually this is the easiest range for new groups.

Can kids do this trip? Yes we coach every step and keep it simple.
