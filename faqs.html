Frequently Asked Questions

What should we bring?

Sunglasses, soft-sole shoes, layered clothing, sunscreen, snacks/drinks, and a small cooler for cleaned fish.

Do we need a fishing license?

Yes. Michigan license required for each angler. Use the DNR eLicense link on our site before arrival.

What is included?

Rods, reels, tackle, bait, and fish cleaning are included.

How many people can come?

Up to 6 guests per trip.

What if weather is unsafe?

Unsafe conditions are rescheduled or refunded per booking terms.

How do we reserve?

Contact us with preferred dates and trip type, then submit a $100 deposit to hold your date.
