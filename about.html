About Dave's Fishing Charters

We run practical, safety-first charter trips designed to put fish in the boat while keeping the day smooth for every skill level.

  • USCG Master Captain Dave Maynard II
  • Up to 6 anglers per trip (family and mixed-skill groups welcome)
  • All tackle provided
  • Fish cleaning included
  • Family and beginner friendly

Captain Focus

Every trip is planned around wind, temp breaks, and bite timing instead of generic routes. You get clear instruction, active support at the rail, and a calm process from launch to dock.

Goal: safe trips, clean communication, and memorable catches.
